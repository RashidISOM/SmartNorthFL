Welcome to Pantry Pal



What is Pantry Pal?

Anybody can use this website. It can be used by people trying to access free food in their area and need to see what's in stock. It can be used by people looking for a food pantry to donate to. It can be used by people to keep up to date on their pantry's needs. Lastly, it can be used by the food pantries themselves to keep and inventory and send notifications.


Find Out More

How do I find a pantry?

Find a pantry in your area with no need to sign in. Just enter your zipcode and then you can look not just at what the pantries in your area offer, but what they are in need of as well. Connect with a pantry in your area and donate. If you sign up for notifications you will be alerted via email when a pantry is in need of certain products or support as we as any urgent messages.
