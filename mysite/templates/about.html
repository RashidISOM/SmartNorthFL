About Us



How We Did It

This project is the result of a five week competition sponsored by Techpoint. We were tasked with developing a tool to help non-profits in Indiana during the current pandemic. We reached out to food pantries in our state to determine what problems they anticipate moving forward and found that many pantries were not taking advantage of the technology available to them. We began listing features that we thought would help local food banks connect and stay organized with the public. From there we sketched out what we wanted the website to look like and began our process of development, testing, and reiteration. Now we are proud to present our solution for Indiana non-profits!
